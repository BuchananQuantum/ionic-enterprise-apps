Komed Health Telecommunications

August, 2021 by Ionic AppFlow

Traditional methods of communication such as phone calls or email can be insecure and unreliable due to factors such as network congestion or dropped calls. In addition, these methods can be time consuming when trying to coordinate care for multiple patients simultaneously. Secure chat apps provide an alternative solution that is both more efficient and secure than traditional methods of communication. With a secure chat app, hospital staff can quickly communicate with one another regardless of location without having to worry about the security of their communications.

The Company

Komed Health is transforming healthcare communication to help doctors, nurses, and other healthcare workers deliver best-in-class patient care.

The company was co-founded by Marc Bornträger and Luiza Dobre, who were motivated after visiting several hospitals and speaking with doctors and nurses. They saw the antiquated technology being used by these otherwise state-of-the-art healthcare facilities – a problem which Komed Health set out to solve.

Komed Health’s platform enables healthcare workers and their institutions to better communicate with one another through a secure real-time messaging system. This not only improves patient care but can also save lives.

The company is based in Zurich, Switzerland – a global center for medical innovation – making them ideally placed to develop this much needed solution. And given that the global market for enterprise communications is forecasted to grow from $130 billion in 2017 to $194 billion by 2021*, it’s clear that there is a lot of potential for Komed Health’s platform.

The Challenge

Komed Health saw first-hand how outdated legacy IT systems were negatively impacting healthcare organizations. Inefficient communication between nurses, doctors, and higher-ups wasn’t just making it hard for healthcare workers to share information; it was resulting in less than stellar patient care.

Marc and Luiza noted that many hospitals were using communication platforms such as Slack or WhatsApp. The problem was that the data stored on these platforms was not secure, and certainly didn’t meet the standards for privacy regulations in a hospital environment. Additionally, these platforms weren’t able to integrate with the existing healthcare IT infrastructure so that electronic medical records, lab results, information systems, and more could be shared easily.

This is where Komed Health came in – they created a messaging platform specifically for hospitals called MediLink. MediLink is HIPAA compliant and can easily integrate with any existing healthcare IT system so that nurses can quickly access patient data without having to leave their workstation. Doctors can also communicate with one another more efficiently by sharing lab results or treatment plans directly through MediLink instead of relying on insecure email communications or phone calls which often result in misunderstandings due to poor audio quality or transcription errors.

The Strategy

Now, let’s dive into the benefits of a cross-platform development approach. First and foremost, it’s important to remember that not all users are created equal. Your app needs to be able to cater to users on different devices, with different screen sizes, running on different versions of Android or iOS. Cross-platform development helps you do just that by allowing you to build once and deploy everywhere.

Not only does this save time and money in the long run, but it also ensures that your app looks great and performs well no matter what device your customer is using. Additionally, developing for multiple platforms can actually lead to increased discoverability – as people search for apps in their respective app stores based on platform (iOS vs Android), a cross-platform strategy gives you a better chance of being found.

When considering how best to build an effective healthcare communication platform, Komed Health first had to decide between native or cross-platform app development approaches. While native devices are known to build the richest user experience, at the time it simply wasn’t in the budget for Komed Health. That quickly led them to pursue a cross–platform development approach instead, one that enabled their team to build for iOS and Android using web technologies without sacrificing any quality from a software or hardware perspectiv . Next ,Komed Health wanted to find a development firm that their existing team could communicate to easily. Already well – versed in Angular, the Komed Health team saw value in Ionic Enterprise Apps as their contractor ; which not only allowed Komed to build using Angular, but also React Vue or JavaScript. Having the flexibility to use different frameworks for multiple projects was something The Komed Health team greatly valued ....

Lastly, Komed Health sought a pure web approach to app development that easily allowed code to be shared between their web and native platforms. The hope was to operate from one codebase that provided Komed Health with everything they needed in terms of components, functionality, and theming. Ideally, this web-first approach would simplify the app development process for Komed Health, while still resulting in a quality app that was on par with the performance of other native apps.

The Selection

When it comes to cross-platform mobile app development, there are a few key players in the space. React Native and Native Script are two of the most popular options, but Ionic Enterprise Apps is quickly gaining ground. Komed Health, a health tech company, recently evaluated all three platforms to see which would be the best fit for their needs.

After considering factors such as performance, cost-effectiveness, and ease of use, Komed Health found that Ionic Enterprise Apps was the ideal platform for them. Ionic’s wide range of components and plugins makes it easy to create high-quality apps without requiring outside developer talent. Additionally, Ionic’s growing community means that there is plenty of support available if you need it.

At a fraction of the cost of developing an app natively with React Native or Native Script, Ionic provides great value for companies looking to get started with cross-platform development. If you’re on the fence about which platform to choose for your next project, consider giving Ionic Enterprise a call.
