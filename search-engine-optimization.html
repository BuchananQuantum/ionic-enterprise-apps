Search Engine Optimization

Search engine optimization, or "SEO" for short, is a technique that can be used on webpages to improve site visibility amongst search engines.

Why Optimize for Search?

When a website is optimized for SEO, it means that the site's creator has taken measures to make sure the page appears as high up as possible in search engine results pages (SERPs), based on specific keywords or phrases. Request an Enterprise SEO audit and plan a strategy session!

Exposure from SEO

For businesses, improved website visibility often leads to an increase in traffic and more customers. In addition, businesses who optimize their websites typically see a decrease in their advertising costs because they no longer have to rely as much on paid ads to reach new customers.

Google doesn't read English!

Google reads schemas and data models. When you present these elements for indexing with Google you want to have integrated schema’s for your business type along with every metric for your businesses operation outlined with Item scopes & item props. Simply put, google doesn’t read English, after we define your metadata such as Keywords, Page Titles, and Descriptions, Ionic Enterprise will define your business model and logic to determine the best strategy for your online success.

People, Places, Things.

The internet is sorted into three categories. Ionic Enterprise SEO leverages the organization of data schemas within our codebase to define the 5 main questions we learn in life. Who, what where, when and why. The 5 w’s, we all learned in school are the simplistic approach to defining the businesses goals, advertising at the proper targeted audience along with giving us a base to start your SEO Journey! Ionic Enterprise SEO evaluates the audience, competition and current placement within the search engines to put together a SEO strategy leveraging our suite of services.
